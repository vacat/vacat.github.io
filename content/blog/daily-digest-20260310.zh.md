### 10. 编码代理能否通过"洁净室"实现重新许可开源代码？

[Can Coding Agents Relicense Open Source Through a ‘Clean Room’ Implementation of Code?](https://simonwillison.net/2026/Mar/5/chardet/) — **daringfireball.net** · 1 天前 · ⭐ 23/30
